Free UK personal finance tools that work entirely in your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no selling.

Why we built this

Most personal finance decisions in the UK shouldn't be hard. How much pension should I contribute? What happens to my Child Benefit? How does salary sacrifice actually work? The answers are out there, but they're buried in jargon, gated behind email sign-ups, or designed to steer you towards a product.

My Pay Picture exists to give you a clear, honest answer in two minutes — then get out of your way. No agenda, no sales funnel, no data harvesting. Just the numbers, explained in plain English.

How we earn your trust

🔒

Your data never leaves your device

Every calculation runs locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server, stored in a database, or shared with anyone. Close the tab and it's gone.

👤

No sign-up, ever

No email, no password, no "create an account to see your results". You open a tool and use it. That's it.

🚧

Not financial advice

We're upfront about what this is: illustrative estimates based on 2025/26 UK tax rates. For decisions that matter, we'll always point you to a qualified adviser.

🎯

No hidden agenda

We don't sell financial products, take referral commissions, or steer you towards any provider. The tools are free. The answers are honest.

Accuracy and rates

All tools use current 2025/26 UK tax rates, thresholds, and allowances. We update them every April when the new tax year starts. Rates are sourced directly from GOV.UK and HMRC publications.

If you spot something that doesn't look right, please let us know at hello@mypaypicture.com.
