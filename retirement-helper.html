£

Average residential care in the UK costs £35,000–£55,000/year. Home care is typically lower. This is added on top of your base withdrawal from the care age onwards.

Care cost cap (from October 2025)

The government's planned care cost cap of £86,000 has been delayed. Currently there is no cap on personal care costs. Local authority support is means-tested — assets above £23,250 must be self-funded in England.

Important caveats
