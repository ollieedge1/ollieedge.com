What to do next

Key deadlines, what to gather, and where to go from here.

Gather these before filing

  • P60 — shows your gross pay and tax paid
  • P11D — if you received taxable benefits
  • Bank interest statements — if above allowance
  • Rental records — rent and expenses
  • Dividend statements — if you received any
  • Pension certificates — for higher rate relief
  • Gift Aid records — for higher rate relief
  • UTR number — your 10-digit taxpayer reference

First time filing?

Register at gov.uk/register-for-self-assessment by 5 October 2025. You’ll get your UTR within 10 days.
